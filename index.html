About

A student-led organization aimed at teens interested in exploring the diverse pathways of healthcare! San Jose Teens for Healthcare is a chapter which hopes bring topics about heathcare to students who wish to pursue a career or explore the heath sector. We offer webinars that provide useful information and the oppurtunity to speak or contact professionals in the health sector.
